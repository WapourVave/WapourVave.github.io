About Me

So, you thought you'd just stumble along this page without me noticing? Well it don't work like that round these parts! My name is Eden, a video games enthusiast and developer in training, I enjoy both playing video games with people and the concept of making a video game others will love to play!

3DS Max

3D asset development optimized for game engine's and scene creation is what I specialize in, creating high quality assets and enviroments by day, charcter rigging, skinning and animating by night. There's never a moment of the day were I don't have to have 3DS Max staring at me from my task bar, waiting...

Unreal Engine 4

When it comes to Unreal 4, I have a large array of skills varying between Blueprint development, sequence and character animation, scene development, practically everything you need for a good designer to get building and prototyping for a game concept. You name a concept and I can probably cook it up in a few hours.

Unreal Development Kit

While knowledge remains limited, UDK was the testing grounds for my first level within the Unreal Engine and taught me design philosophies I still use now, as well as showcasing just how far I have come now in terms of skill level. UDK may be old, but the lessons I learnt about level design were valuable.

Photoshop

My skills within Photoshop range primarily with assisting in bigger projects, primarily image manipulation, creation of seamless textures and UV Mapping. Whether it be creating planning documentation for a building or a skydome for a video game, I've got Photoshops back as long as Photoshop has mine.

Game Maker

My skills in Game Maker are simple, I can create video games, as the name suggests. Need sprite based assets? Yeah, I have them. Need a platformer with a 2D momentum based physics engine? Been there, done that. Need a game with.. You know were I'm going with this. I make games alright, its what I do.
