About Me

So, you thought you'd just stumble along this page without me noticing? Well it don't work like that round these parts! My name is Eden, a video games enthusiast and developer in training, I enjoy both playing video games with people and the concept of making a video game others will love to play!

Unreal Development Kit

When it comes to UDK, I have had very little experience with the engine a long time ago. Regardless, its interesting to look at old projects to gain a perspective of how much design knowledge can be accumulated over the years. The images above showcase an assignment in which we were tasked with building a 3D level in UDK, the level was completed within a day and was very simplistic in design. It featured a mirrored map with 2 hallways on both sides, a power weapon in the middle of the map and rocket boots under the water (which allowed players to get the power weapon on the map that way). In terms of design, it taught me an understanding of how video game engines work, what they require of me and what I need to make to create a fun and engaging experience. These lessons stuck with me and were expanded upon into Unreal Engine 4.
